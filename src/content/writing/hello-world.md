This is the first entry on my new site. I built it because I wanted one place
that holds the things I care about &mdash; the projects I have shipped, the
ideas I am thinking through in writing, and a running photo journal of
wherever I happen to be.

## Why a personal site, in 2026

It is mine. The platform cannot change the rules, throttle the reach, or
decide my work belongs behind a paywall. Posts here exist as plain markdown
files on disk; if I ever want to leave, I take a folder with me.

## What lives here

- **Projects.** Things I have built. Some shipped, some half-finished.
- **Writing.** Notes on whatever I am chewing on this month.
- **Journal.** Photos with a few sentences of context. Closer to a diary
  than a portfolio.

More to come.
